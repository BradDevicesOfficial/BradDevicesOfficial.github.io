About Brad Devices

Brad Devices is the hardware division of BradVerse — a vertically integrated company created to design, build, secure and scale devices and systems for a new generation. Our founder, Festus Bradley Nyadimo (The Architect), focuses on building an entire ecosystem: silicon, OS, AI, cloud and devices — engineered to work together, at scale.

Founding Philosophy

We believe in “Open Core, Proprietary Edge”: publish the foundations where community accelerates innovation, and protect the crown-jewels (NeuroSense models, BradXon silicon, BradSec hardware stacks) that deliver unique value.

The Six Proprietary Pillars

EROE

Eco-Render Optimization Engine — intelligently balances CPU/GPU/NPU workloads to maximize battery and performance.

AMF

Adaptive Memory Flow — predictive memory tiering and prefetching for snappy apps and reduced power draw.

PPB

Proximity Performance Boost — secure workload sharing between linked Brad devices in close proximity.

OmniLink

A fast, secure protocol for file sync, device pairing, and Continuum Flow features.

Continuum Flow

Seamless task transfer across devices — start on phone, continue on laptop; universal clipboard & session handoff.

NeuroSense

Our adaptive AI core: personalization, predictive UX, image processing, security monitoring and local inference.

Silicon: BradCore & BradXon

BradCore™: ARMv9-based multi-cluster SoCs with asymmetrical Phoenix (big) and Falcon (efficiency) cores, integrated BradNPU and BradFx GPU. Target: phones, tablets, standard laptops.

BradXon™: Cognitive C.P.U. for the bleeding edge — ANF fabric, AIMPs for model execution, photonic interconnect concepts and micro-fluidic cooling ideas. Target: high-performance servers, research nodes and hyper-reality compute.

BradSec — Security as a Product

We bake security into silicon and the OS: hardware root-of-trust, secure boot chain, cryptographic signing for all modules, HSM/private key protection, tamper detection, and an invigilator-friendly Classroom Control Unit (CCU) for exam integrity.

BradLabs & R&D

BradLabs prototypes devices, performs silicon bring-up, stress tests power & thermal, and runs privacy/security audits. We also maintain BradBounties for community audits and certified driver development.

Open Core + Proprietary Edge

We plan to open key kernel-level components and APIs (to grow developers & drivers) while keeping NeuroSense models, BradXon, and BradSec proprietary for competitive advantage.
